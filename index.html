Privacy Policy

MJGA Apps

Last updated: 2026-04-05

Overview

MJGA Apps develops local utility applications for Android. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our apps.

Information We Collect

The App itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

Storage Access

The App requests access to your device storage solely to read and play video files selected by you. No video files or file paths are transmitted outside of your device.

Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data to provide personalized ads. For details, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob — Advertising (Privacy Policy)
  • Google ExoPlayer — Video playback library (no data collection)
  • VideoLAN VLC — Video playback library (no data collection)

Data Security

Since the App does not collect or transmit personal data, there is no risk of data breach from our side.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
